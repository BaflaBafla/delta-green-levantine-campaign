# Mechanical FAQ — The Levantine Engine (compact)

This FAQ clarifies common rules to speed play. Copy to the table cheat-sheet.

1) Pact vs Static — timing
- Pact spends: applied immediately when a player accepts the Handler's cost. If a spend causes a visible effect, resolve that effect immediately.
- Event-driven Pact increases (seal destroyed, mass casualty): apply immediately when the event's outcome is known, not at end of scene.
- Static Die increases: apply as soon as an action creates searchable/traceable evidence (e.g., leaving fingerprints, a witness seeing you). Large scene-wide effects (newspaper story) apply at end of scene during debrief.

2) WP economy (Willpower Points)
- Starting WP: characters begin with WP = POW / 5 (rounded down), minimum 1.
- Regeneration: 1 WP per full in-game day of rest, or 1 WP recovered during a standard downtime (~1 week). No automatic per-session refresh.
- Spending: Artifact active abilities cost WP as written. The Handler can grant one emergency WP recovery (narrative justification) per arc.

3) Group Bond mechanics
- Projection: To project SAN into the Group Bond, the acting player declares intention and roleplays an interpersonal outburst. Mechanically: roll SAN as normal; if accepted, move the chosen SAN loss amount into the Group Bond instead of the character — update the shared pool. If the player refuses, they take SAN normally.
- When Group Bond hits 0: immediate social fracture — the Handler prompts two characters to roleplay a significant ideological split. This imposes -10% to team cooperative rolls until repaired.

4) Artifact fragility & handling
- Aleppo fragments: each scene handled without professional conservation causes 1d4% structural damage. At 0% a fragment is destroyed.
- Repair attempts: Conservator roll (Archaeology or similar) — success reduces accumulated damage by 1d6% and costs time and equipment. Failure risks accidental loss.

5) Static Die on 10 table (thematic suggestions)
- Nablus (Arc1): Newspaper arrests, villagers blamed.
- Golan (Arc2): Military investigation, satellite imagery flagged.
- Gaza (Arc3): Tunnel collapse with social media video.
- Jerusalem (Arc4): Museum security footage surfaces; religious press and international security attention.

6) Examples
- Example 1 (Pact spend): Player spends Pact +1 to reroll a failed Stealth check; Pact increases immediately. Static increases only if the reroll causes public action (e.g., shooting someone) that creates evidence.
- Example 2 (Artifact use): Player uses Brass Fish active to sense creatures through earth (Pact +1, WP spend). Apply Pact immediately; if used in public or near witnesses, Static +1.
