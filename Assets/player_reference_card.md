# Player Reference — Quick Cheat (text)

THE PACT TRACK (0–10)
- 0 Dormant: peace possible
- 1–2 Quiet: stray dreams
- 3–4 Murmur: start zone (campaign begins at 3)
- 5–6 Pressing: reality warps locally
- 7–8 Manifestation: avatars possible
- 9 Threshold: seals crack
- 10 Breach: egregores enter

SPEND PACT
- +1: Reroll a failed check
- +2: Lesser unnatural effect
- +3: Major working
- +4: Egregore bargain (Handler sets cost)

STATIC DIE (d10)
- Tracks mundane attention (witnesses, evidence)
- Start at 1. On 10, roll d4 complication, then reset to 5.
- Increases on evidence left, public Pact use, failed cover checks.

GROUP BOND
- Shared SAN buffer equal to average CHA
- Players can project SAN into it (roleplay + accept mechanical shift)
- At 0: team fractures; penalties to cooperation until repaired

HANDY REMINDERS
- Pact spends are immediate; Static increments may be immediate or applied at scene end depending on source.
- Fragile artifacts degrade if handled carelessly.
- Session Zero & safety first. Use X-Card and check-ins.
